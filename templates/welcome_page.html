Welcome to my website

This website's purpose is to collect student feedback to potentially improve GIC in the future.

You can explore through the site using the links above and discover information such as why collecting students' data and experience at the college is helpful and important and also where you can submit your experience to help improve the college's services

  • Information page: This page is where you can learn more about the reasoning for collecting data as well as the benefits it provides.
  • Data collection page: This page is where you can share your information as well as experience at the college, any feedback would be greatly appreciated.

Thank you for taking time to explore this site and possibly giving feedback, any information you can give will be very helpful, thank you.
